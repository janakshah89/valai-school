How Do We Use Cookies?

  • Cookies are small files that reside on your hard drive after seeking permission. Cookies help us to analyze web traffic and let us know when you visited our website. Cookies also allow web applications to respond to your requests as an individual thus making application tailor-made for your needs, likes and dislikes. At the same time, cookies gather and remember information about your preferences.
  • We also use traffic log cookies to identify which particular page of the website is under use. This information helps us to improve our web pages according to user needs. This information is used only for statistical analyses and the data is removed from the system as soon as the analysis is completed.
  • In overall, cookies help us to provide you a better website by monitoring which pages are useful for you and which pages are not. Cookies do not provide us access to your system or any other information about you than the data you voluntarily share with us.
  • You are liberty to choose or decline cookies. Most of the web browsers automatically accept cookies but you can decline cookies by changing settings of your web browser. This may, however, be able to bar you from taking full advantage of the website.
